Easy Recipes

EasyRecipes was my first venture into app development, and it served as an exciting and rewarding learning experience. The app allows users to enter and save their own recipes, complete with photos to make each dish visually appealing. Its clean and intuitive interface makes it easy to organize and access recipes, effectively functioning as a personalized digital recipe book. This project introduced me to core development concepts, from creating user-friendly forms to managing data storage for saved recipes. It was a fun and inspiring way to combine my technical skills with a practical, everyday application.
