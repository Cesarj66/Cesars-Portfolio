Introduction

My name is Cesar Jasso. I am a senior at CSUF majoring in Computer Science. I will be graduating in the Spring of 2025. I never had thoughts of becoming a programmer, not even in my first year of college. I was always interested in technology and would disassemble electronics to see the parts that make them work, but I never thought of it as more than just curiosity. It wasn't until my second year of college that I took a programming class and I was hooked. I realized that programming was not some abstract concept, but a way to create something from nothing. It was like magic. I started to learn more about programming and I immediately knew that I wanted to create programs of my own. That first programming class was the first time I was able to say that I created something that was entirely my own and I never looked back since.
